What we do

The Apollo Foundation is a non-profit organization founded by High School students that work to provide better and more enhanced education to underprivileged students around the globe. We do so through our dedicated volunteers, exceptional international chapters, compassionate sponsors/partners, and our ambitious management team.

Volunteers

We handpick affectionate individuals who are excited to help underprivileged students around the world and assign them fun and engaging activities that furthermore expand our foundation.

Chapters

Passionate individuals spread our foundation through forming chapters in their communities, schools, and regions. Our valuable chapters stop at nothing to provide for the underprivileged.

Sponsors

Our amazing outreach team reaches out to companies and organizations that are interested in our cause. These sponsors help our foundation in numerous areas such as funding for major events.

Partners

Our partners play a key role in supporting our volunteers and foundation. Partnerships are purely for helping two causes efficiently and avidly, while also gaining new fundraising opportunities.

Our Projects

The Apollo Foundation, along with our dedicated volunteers, has partnered with the Roshni Learning Center, to provide such enhanced education to underprivileged students from two schools located in Bihar, India. The Roshni Learning Center was founded by a group of ambitious scholars who came from American and Indian backgrounds, with an overall aim to help the impoverished.

The Situation

Even though thousands of government schools are created across the country, many students in India do not attend school. These students have pressing issues in areas such as the distance of travel, issues with the family, and lack of proper materials for the innumerable amount of students.

Our Goal

Money and resources gathered by the Apollo Foundation go straight to the Roshni Learning Center, in which, Roshni provide students with free stationery school bags, lunches, online/material learning tools, and free tutoring.

How we do it

The Roshni Learning Center spends time looking for passionate individuals willing to take time out of their lives to provide free tutoring for these students. The Apollo Foundation aims to be a large supporting figure at Roshni through funding, online tutoring, and moral support through various campaigns.

Volunteer with us

The Apollo Foundation aims to develop curriculums, raise funds, and foster awareness for impoverished schools and children through the support of our volunteers. Volunteers are also able to receive volunteer rewards such as the exceptional PVSA award. We highly encourage individuals of any age to help our cause. If interested, please join our Discord server as this is where we communicate with our volunteers and supporters.

Virtual Support

We are looking for individuals interested in supporting our foundation online. Online volunteers spread our foundation in different areas of the internet by means of advertisements and event outreach, as well as online tutoring. All at home!

Volunteering

Having a strong volunteer team on the ground is vital. We have opportunities for tutoring, managing fundraisers, finding sponsors/partners, and much more. All opportunities give you potential to grow in numerous aspects such as leadership and communication.

Chapters

We highly encourage ambitious individuals around the world to start chapters in their region. Doing so will give you and your peers the opportunity to lead and get first-hand experience in business, while also building your overall resume.
